Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Fettuccine
  • 1 lb Shrimp
  • 4 clove Garlic
  • ½ cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • ¼ Juice of Lemon Juice from 1/4 of Lemon
  • ¼ Lemon Zest from 1/4 of Lemon
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large pot boiling salted water, cook fettuccine pasta until al dente, drain well then set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Cook garlic until fragrant for 2 minutes.
  3. Melt butter in the same pan then cooks shrimp. Stir occasionally until the shrimp turn opaque white with some pink for about 4-6 minutes.
  4. Add lemon juice, lemon zest, parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper. Stir until combined and the parmesan cheese melted.
  5. Transfer the fettuccine into a pan, stir until evenly distributed.
  6. Garnish with parsley before serving.
  7. Enjoy!
Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 45 minutes Servings: 5 people

Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-inch) round of pizza dough
  • 3 tbsp tomato sauce
  • Some good olive oil
  • 4 or 5 basil leaves, torn into pieces
  • 2 3/4 oz fresh mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to the highest temperature possible. Place a pizza stone or tile son the middle rack of the oven and let it heat up for 1 hour.
  2. Put the sauce in the center of the dough round and use the back of a spoon to spread it evenly over the pizza, stopping about a half an inch from the edge. Drizzle a little olive oil over the sauce and scatter the basil on top. (Roberta's puts the basil under the cheese so that the heat from the wood-fired oven doesn't incinerate it.)
  3. Break the mozzarella into several large chunks and distribute it over the pizza. Bake the pizza until the crust is golden brown and bubbly.
  4. Enjoy!
    5. Makes 1 (12-inch) pizza.

Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone
  • 2 cups double cream
  • 2 cups strong coffee
  • 1 cup kahlua
  • 24 u lady fingers
  • cocoa powder for garnish

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale and creamy.
  2. Add mascarpone to the egg yolk mixture and mix until smooth.
  3. Whip double cream until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Make a strong cup of coffee and let it cool. Add Kahlua and mix.
  6. Dip ladyfingers into the coffee-Kahlua mixture.
  7. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in a serving dish.
  8. Spread half of the mascarpone cream mixture over the ladyfingers.
  9. Repeat the layers with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone cream mixture.
  10. Chill for 2 hours
  11. Before serving, sift cocoa powder over the top for garnish.
  12. Slice and serve chilled.
  13. Enjoy!
